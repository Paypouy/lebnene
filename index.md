# Lebnene — Lebanese Arabic for Work & Friends

An open-source Lebanese Arabic course for learners who can already read Arabic
script and want spoken Lebanese for daily life, work, and friendships in
Lebanon.

The course is organized as a 16-week path to A2 across 32 lessons, with Anki
decks and assessments generated from the lesson material in this repository.

## Start here

- [Module 1 — Foundations](lessons/module-1/)
- [Module 2 — Daily life](lessons/module-2/)
- [Module 3 — Past and future](lessons/module-3/)
- [Module 4 — Work and social](lessons/module-4/)
- [Assessments and progress tracking](assessments/)

## Course features

- Scenario-based lessons with dialogue, vocab, drills, and role-play
- Auto-generated Anki decks from lesson vocab tables
- Progress checklists, monologue prompts, and an A2 exit assessment
