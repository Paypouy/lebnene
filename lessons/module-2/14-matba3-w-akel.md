## Drills
1. Menu run: real Lebanese menu (photo); student orders 3 courses for 2 people.
2. Comparatives: tayyeb→atyab, 7elo→a7la, rkhis→arkhas, applied to dishes.
3. Hunger states: ju3én/sheb3én/manné ju3én Q&A.

## Role-play variations
- Group dinner: order for 3 with one vegetarian (ma byékol la7mé).
- The host insists you eat more (kol! kol!) — decline gracefully: walla shbe3et, sa7tén!
- Complication: wrong dish arrives — soft version: 3an iznak, ne7na talabna metabbal, mish baba ghannouj.
