# Lesson 12 — The serviis

> Culture: a *serviis* is a shared taxi on a flexible route; you state the
> destination through the window, the driver nods or drives off. A fare may
> be doubled: "serviisén" (two fares) for longer rides.

## Drills
1. Window shout: teacher = driver; student states 6 destinations with 3a/3al-.
2. fi/ma fi: fi 3aj2a lyom? ma fi mahal — 8 rounds.
3. Polite commands stack: 3mol ma3ruf + nazzelné/khod yamin/stanna shwayy.

## Role-play variations
- Standard ride with price pushback (dialogue).
- Driver chats (recycle L04 interrogation!) while you also navigate.
- Complication: driver goes the wrong way — la2 la2, mish hon! 2elt 3al-7amra!
