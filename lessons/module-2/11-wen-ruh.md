# Lesson 11 — Directions & the city

## Dialogue

| | Arabic | Translit | Deutsch | English |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Daniel | عن إذنك، كيف بروح عالصيدلية؟ | 3an iznak, kif brou7 3al-saydaliyyé? | Entschuldigung, wie komme ich zur Apotheke? | Excuse me, how do I get to the pharmacy? |
| Man | روح دغري، وبعدين خود يمين عند السوبرماركت. | rou7 deghré, w ba3dén khod yamin 3and l-supermarket. | Geh geradeaus, dann rechts beim Supermarkt. | Go straight, then right at the supermarket. |
| Daniel | دغري وبعدين يمين. بعيدة؟ | deghré w ba3dén yamin. b3idé? | Geradeaus, dann rechts. Ist sie weit? | Straight then right. Is it far? |
| Man | لأ، حدّ البنك، قبال الجامع. | la2, 7add l-bank, 2bél l-jéme3. | Nein, neben der Bank, gegenüber der Moschee. | No, next to the bank, opposite the mosque. |
| Daniel | يسلمو كتير! | yeslamo ktir! | Vielen Dank! | Thanks a lot! |

## Drills
1. Map game: hand-drawn map; teacher gives directions, student traces; reverse.
2. Imperative flip: brou7 → rou7! békhod → khod! (8 verbs).
3. Where-is grid: wén l-foren? — 7add l-knisé, wara l-bank…

## Role-play variations
- Lost tourist (dialogue) ×3 destinations.
- Phone navigation: guide the teacher to "your" flat, landmark by landmark — Lebanese style (no street names, all landmarks: 3and l-dekkéné, ba3ed l-knisé…).
