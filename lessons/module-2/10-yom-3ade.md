## Drills
1. Sequence scramble: teacher gives routine steps out of order, student re-tells in order with awwal shé/ba3dén/bil-ékher.
2. Interview: kif byemshé nharak? answered, then student interviews teacher and retells in 3rd person (byfi2… byeshteghel…).
3. Time anchor: every sentence must contain a clock time.

## Role-play variations
- Weekday vs. Saturday: tell both, contrast with bass.
- Remote-work day vs. office day.

## Assignment
- Anki deck 10. Voice note: your full real day, 10+ sentences, sequenced.
- Listening: teacher records THEIR day; student writes the times heard.
