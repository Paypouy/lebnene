# Lesson 16 — Review & Assessment 2

## Part 1 — One day in Beirut (40 min, continuous simulation)
Teacher chains the scenarios with NO resets — one continuous improvised day:
1. Morning: tell the time, you're late (L09) → grab coffee to go (L06)
2. Take a serviis across town, negotiate fare (L12)
3. Ask directions to a new office (L11)
4. Lunch: order for yourself + a colleague (L14)
5. Weather small talk in the elevator (L15)
6. Evening: dekkéné shop with a 6-item list (L13)
7. Phone call: tell a friend about your day using sequencers (L10)

Score each station ✅/🟡/❌ → `assessments/progress-log.md`.

## Part 2 — Recorded monologue #2 (same 3 prompts as week 4)
Save as `assessments/recordings/week08-prompt{1,2,3}.mp3`.
**Listen to week-04 versions together. This comparison is the single most
motivating 10 minutes of the course.**

## Part 3 — Comprehension
3 voice notes at near-native speed: a friend proposing a plan (time+place),
directions, a shop interaction. 2/3 = pass.

## Part 4 — Can-do checklist
`assessments/can-do-module-2.md` together.

## Gate to Module 3
≥5/7 stations ✅/🟡 + comprehension pass + can-do ≥80%.
Module 3 introduces past tense — the biggest grammar jump in the course —
so the M2 base must be automatic, not just familiar.
