# Lesson 29 — The expression toolkit

> These aren't decoration — Lebanese conversation is BUILT from these.
> Using two of them correctly does more for his social standing than a
> month of grammar.

## Dialogue (expressions in the wild)

| | Arabic | Translit | English |
|---|---|---|---|
| Sami | ولك دانيال! شو في ما في؟ | wlek Daniel! shu fi ma fi? | Daniel! What's up? |
| Daniel | والله ولا شي عبالي اليوم… سهرت لساعة تلاتة. | walla wala shé 3abélé lyom… sheret la-sé3a tlété. | Honestly zero energy today… was out till 3. |
| Sami | نيالك! نحنا نمنا الساعة عشرة. | niyélak! ne7na nemna l-sé3a 3ashra. | Lucky you! We slept at ten. |

## Drills
1. Situation→expression: teacher describes 12 micro-situations, student fires the right one instantly.
2. Register check: which can you say to your boss? (3a rasé ✓, t2ebburné ✗✗✗).
3. The kahraba joke: when the power cuts mid-lesson (it will), the student must react in Arabic only.

## Role-play variations
- Friend gathering: teacher + (ideally) one guest native speaker, expressions flying. Student must land ≥5 naturally.

## Assignment
- Anki deck 29. Field work: use 3 expressions on real Lebanese people this week, report reactions.
