# Lesson 31 — Grand review

## The wheel (50 min)
All 24 content scenarios on cards, drawn at random, 3 minutes each,
chained where possible (the doctor visit leads to the pharmacy leads to
calling in sick). Teacher escalates: speaks faster, code-switches,
interrupts, plays difficult.

Target: 10–12 scenarios in the hour. Log every breakdown — those exact
gaps are tomorrow's exam prep.

## Vocabulary sweep (20 min)
Anki "hardest 50" (lowest ease cards across all decks) live with the
teacher: each must be used in an original sentence, not just translated.

## Exam briefing
Walk through `assessments/a2-exit-exam.md` so nothing about the FORMAT is
a surprise — only the content is improvised on the day.

## Assignment
- Anki: full review, all decks.
- Sleep. Seriously — the exam rewards automaticity, and automaticity rewards sleep.
