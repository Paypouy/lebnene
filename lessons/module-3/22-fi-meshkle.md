## Drills
1. What's broken: 10 household items + the right participle (kharbén/ma2tu3/mkassar).
2. lézim sentences: lézim tejé lyom, lézim nsalle7 l-sakhkhén…
3. Pin-the-time: vague answers (bukra, ba3dén) → student extracts a window every time.

## Role-play variations
- Plumber call (dialogue), then electrician (kahraba ma2tu3a + motor questions — very Lebanese).
- Landlord escalation: second call, polite but firm — recycle past tense: ttasalet fik mbéri7 w ma jit!
