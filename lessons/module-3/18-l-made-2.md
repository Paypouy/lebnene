# Lesson 18 — Past tense II: the irregulars you can't avoid

## Grammar point: the big six

| verb | ana | inta | huwwé | henné |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| go | re7et | re7et | ra7 | ra7o |
| come | jit | jit | ija | ijo |
| see | shefet | shefet | shéf | shéfo |
| eat | akalet | akalet | akal | akalo |
| say | 2elet | 2elet | 2al | 2alo |
| be | kenet | kenet | kén | kéno |

**kén** unlocks past descriptions: kén fi 3aj2a (there was traffic),
kenet ta3bén (I was tired).

## Drills
1. Big-six ladder, all persons, speed rounds.
2. Story chain: each adds one past sentence to a growing weekend story; recap from the top each turn.
3. kén-states: kif kenet mbéri7? kenet ta3bén/mabsut/ju3én…

## Role-play variations
- Monday catch-up (dialogue), with real weekend content.
- Tell a travel memory: marra re7et 3a… — 8 sentences minimum.
