# Lesson 24 — Review & Assessment 3

## Part 1 — Narrative control (20 min)
1. **Yesterday in full:** 2 minutes, uninterrupted, past tense (L17–18).
2. **Next week in full:** 2 minutes, ra7-future (L19).
3. **The switch:** teacher calls out "mbéri7!"/"bukra!" mid-story; student must flip tense on the same content. Brutal and effective.

## Part 2 — The phone gauntlet (30 min, all back-to-back / real calls)
1. Book a doctor's appointment, then call back and move it (L20–21).
2. Report a broken water heater, pin a time window (L22).
3. Dispute a wrong charge (L23).
Score ✅/🟡/❌ → progress-log.
