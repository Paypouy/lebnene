# Lesson 17 — Past tense I

## Grammar point: the paradigm (verb daras — to study)

| ana | inta | inté | huwwé | hiyyé | ne7na | inton | henné |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| daras**et** | daras**t** | daras**té** | daras | daras**et** | daras**na** | daras**to** | daras**o** |

> Note the trap: *ana darasET* and *hiyyé darasET* are identical — context
> disambiguates. Negation: **ma darasit shi** patterns or simply ma + verb.

## Drills
1. Paradigm snake on 4 verbs, then negated.
2. Present→past transformation: bedros → daraset; beshteghel → shtaghalet (10 verbs).
3. Alibi game: teacher interrogates about yesterday hour-by-hour; student answers in past, consistently.

## Role-play variations
- "shu 3melet mbéri7?" — truthful answer, 6+ sentences.
- Catch-up with a friend: each tells their (real) yesterday, asks 2 follow-ups.
