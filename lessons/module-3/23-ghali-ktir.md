> Culture: 7abibé between men in a negotiation is normal and softening,
> not romantic. Germans find this one hilarious, then indispensable.

## Drills
1. Haggling script: 2addé? → ghalé ktir! → ékher se3er? → nazzellé yé — run with 6 items, teacher resists.
2. iza-conditionals: iza ma fi badil… iza btnazzellé… 6 builds.
3. Firm-but-warm tone drill: same complaint sentence said angry vs. Lebanese-style (smiling steel). Record both, compare.

## Role-play variations
- Souk haggling for a jacket: target 30% off.
- Restaurant overcharge on the bill (recycles L06/L14).
- Phone version (recycles L20): complain about an internet bill.
