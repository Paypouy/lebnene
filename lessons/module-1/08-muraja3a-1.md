# Lesson 08 — Review & Assessment 1

**Format:** no new material. 60–90 minutes of cycling through everything.

## Part 1 — The gauntlet (30 min)
Run each scenario once, unscripted, teacher in character:
1. Meet a stranger at a work event (L01+L04): greet, names, origin, work, languages.
2. Politeness chain (L02): teacher creates 5 situations, student fires formulas.
3. SIM shop (L03): buy, ask price, take down the number spoken fast.
4. Photo conversation (L05): teacher shows THEIR photos, student asks min haida?
5. Café (L06): order for two, get the bill, tip.
6. Phone check-in (L07): shu 3am ta3mel? + daily-routine questions.

Scoring per scenario: ✅ smooth / 🟡 completed with help / ❌ broke down.
Log results in `assessments/progress-log.md`.

## Part 2 — First recorded monologue (10 min)
Record, keep forever (compare at weeks 8, 12, 16):
1. "7ké 3an 7élak" — introduce yourself fully (1–2 min)
2. "shu bta3mel kell yom?" — your routine (1–2 min)
3. "7ké 3an 3éltak" — your family (1–2 min)
Store as `assessments/recordings/week04-prompt{1,2,3}.mp3`.

## Part 3 — Comprehension check (10 min)
Teacher sends 3 WhatsApp voice notes at natural speed combining M1
material. Student writes (in any language) what was said. 2/3 correct = pass.

## Part 4 — Can-do checklist
Tick together — honestly — `assessments/can-do-module-1.md`.

## Gate
**Move to Module 2 if:** ≥4 of 6 gauntlet scenarios ✅/🟡, comprehension
2/3, and can-do ≥80%. Otherwise: one consolidation week (re-drill the ❌
scenarios), then re-test. No shame in it — better here than collapsing in M3.

## Assignment
- Rest week for Anki: reviews only, no new cards.
- Listen to the week-04 recordings once. Note (in German, fine) what felt hardest.
