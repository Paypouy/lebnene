# Lesson 07 — The present tense engine

**Scenario:** You can answer "shu 3am ta3mel?" (what are you doing?) and
"shu bta3mel kell yom?" (what do you do every day?) — and hear the
difference.

## Warm-up
Café ordering blitz; badd- conjugation backwards (teacher gives form,
student names person).

## Grammar point: the whole system on one verb

**yeshrab (drink), stem -shrab-:**

| | habitual (b-) | progressive (3am) |
|---|---|---|
| ana | beshrab | 3am beshrab |
| inta | bteshrab | 3am bteshrab |
| inté | bteshrabé | 3am bteshrabé |
| huwwé | byeshrab | 3am byeshrab |
| hiyyé | bteshrab | 3am bteshrab |
| ne7na | mneshrab | 3am mneshrab |
| inton | bteshrabo | 3am bteshrabo |
| henné | byeshrabo | 3am byeshrabo |

- Habitual = German Präsens for routines: **beshrab 2ahwé kell yom.**
- 3am = German „gerade": **3am beshrab 2ahwé** (right now).
- Negation: **ma** before everything: ma beshrab, ma 3am beshrab.

## Dialogue

| | Arabic | Translit | Deutsch | English |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Sami | شو عم تعمل؟ | shu 3am ta3mel? | Was machst du gerade? | What are you doing? |
| Daniel | عم بدرس عربي. | 3am bedros 3arabé. | Ich lerne gerade Arabisch. | I'm studying Arabic. |
| Sami | كل يوم بتدرس؟ | kell yom btedros? | Lernst du jeden Tag? | Do you study every day? |
| Daniel | إي، بدرس نص ساعة كل يوم، بس اليوم عم بدرس أكتر. | é, bedros noss sé3a kell yom, bass lyom 3am bedros aktar. | Ja, ich lerne jeden Tag eine halbe Stunde, aber heute lerne ich mehr. | Yes, half an hour daily, but today I'm studying more. |
| Sami | ليش؟ | lésh? | Warum? | Why? |
| Daniel | لأنّو عندي امتحان مع الأستاذ! | la2anno 3andé imti7an ma3 l-istéz! | Weil ich eine Prüfung beim Lehrer habe! | Because I have a test with the teacher! |

## Drills
1. **Conjugation snake:** one verb, around the persons, then 3am-forms, then negated. Verbs: yedros, yeshrab, yé2kol, yshuf.
2. **Habit vs. now:** teacher shows a phrase card ("kell yom" / "halla2") + verb → student builds the right form.
3. **Why-because chain:** lésh 3am tedros? la2anno… — 6 different reasons.

## Role-play variations
- Phone call: shu 3am ta3mel? — describe your actual current scene.
- Describe your real daily routine in 5 sentences (preview of L10).
- Complication: Sami mishears — student must repeat with mish… correction: la2, mish 3am beshrab, 3am bedros!
