# Recorded monologue protocol

**Every 4 weeks. Same prompts. 1–2 minutes each. Never deleted.**
Filename: `recordings/weekNN-promptN.mp3`

1. **7ké 3an 7élak** — introduce yourself fully (from week 4)
2. **Shu bta3mel kell yom?** — your routine (from week 4)
3. **7ké 3an 3éltak** — your family (from week 4)
4. **Kif kén weekendak?** — last weekend, past tense (from week 12)

Rules: no notes, no script, one take. Stumbling is data, not failure.
After each session: listen to the 4-weeks-ago set together and note, in
one line each: pronunciation / speed / range / confidence.
