# A2 Exit Exam — full protocol

**Duration:** ~90 min. **Examiner:** teacher (+ guest native speaker if possible — calibrates against non-teacher speech).

## Part 1 — Monologues (20 min) — 25%
All 4 standard prompts, recorded as week-16 set.
Rubric per prompt, 1–5 each on: fluency · comprehensibility · range · accuracy.
Pass: average ≥3.0, no prompt below 2.5.

## Part 2 — Interaction gauntlet (25 min) — 30%
5 scenario cards drawn blind from the 24. Teacher plays "hostile mode":
fast speech, code-switching, interruptions, one curveball per scenario.
Pass: 4/5 scenarios reach their goal (the order placed, the time pinned,
the refund won), help allowed once per scenario.

## Part 3 — Native listening (20 min) — 25%
Two unseen 60–90s native clips (one conversational, one monologue —
podcast/vox-pop). 5 questions each, answered in any language.
Pass: 6/10.

## Part 4 — Real-world portfolio (10 min) — 10%
Evidence review: the real phone call, ≥3 shop/café/serviis missions,
the expressions field report. Pass: complete and credible.

## Part 5 — Tense switch (5 min) — 10%
One story, teacher calls "mbéri7!/bukra!/halla2!" every ~20s. Pass:
survives 3 minutes with mostly-correct switches.

**Overall pass: ≥70% weighted + Parts 2 and 3 individually passed.**
Failure mode is normal: retake only the failed parts after 2 weeks.
